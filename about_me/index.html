About Me

My name is Mateusz Szymoński.

I work as a Technical Artist at CD PROJEKT RED and as a Software Engineer at Sticky Piston Studios, the startup I co-founded.

  • I’m passionate about Computer Science, with a strong focus on game development - particularly in Technical Art, Computer Graphics, and Engine Programming.

  • Beyond games, I also work on Web Application Development, bringing the same creativity and problem-solving mindset to building scalable systems and platforms.

  • As a self-taught 3D Artist, I specialize in prop modeling and environment art.

I graduated from Warsaw University of Technology with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, earning the highest distinction for my thesis: “Design and Implementation of a Rust-based Computer Game Engine”.

Outside of work, you’ll often find me at game jams and hackathons, or experimenting with self-hosted and automation projects.

Agent-based modelling of car platooning for traffic optimization

Unity C#

A scientific research paper which I was co-author of, published in 2021 4th International Symposium on Agents, Multi-Agent Systems and Robotics (ISAMSR)

Addressing the topic of gasoline consumption reduction thanks to the implementation of a platooning system for autonomous vehicles moving on the highways.

For the purpose of this study, a state machine system of distributed agents was created to simulate the communication and cooperation of the vehicles and to calculate the air resistance factor reduction.

DOI: 10.1109/ISAMSR53229.2021.9567855

Temple of Ylnir

Unity Blender Substance Painter Substance Designer Photoshop

Additionally, a small manager tool allowing for controlling the demo playback was created.
